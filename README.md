Protection and Strength – The shield itself is a universal symbol of defense and safety. It implies that whoever bears the shield is prepared to defend themselves or their values.

The claw is animal spirit (honey badger) in this case which  embodies qualities of bravery.


G – Growth

Represents personal development, learning from experiences, and improving continuously.

Focuses on building skills and resilience over time.

R – Resilience

The ability to bounce back from setbacks, challenges, or failures.

Staying strong mentally and emotionally when things get tough.

I – Integrity

Being honest, consistent, and true to your values.

Doing the right thing even when it’s hard or no one is watching.

T – Tenacity

Determination to keep going, even when progress is slow or obstacles appear.

Persistence and never giving up until the goal is reached.

Overall Meaning of "Grit"

Grit is the combination of passion and perseverance toward long term goals.
